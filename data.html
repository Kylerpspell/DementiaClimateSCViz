Alzheimers Disease Registry:

This data was collected by the Arnold School of Public Health and is the nations most comprehensive Registry of its kind. The data I used in my project was:
  • YODX: Year of diagnosis
  • DX_Age: Age of diagnosis
  • Dementia: Type of dementia
  • zip0: 5 digit zipcode
I used ~98,000 records from the registry to populate my visualizations. Link to their page

Climate Data:

This data was collected by using the Farmers Almanac Weather History Page. I used the zipcode data to populate a 20 year history of climate data for each record in the database. The data I used were:
  • High Temp: The high tempurature recorded on the 15th of each month for the past 20 years. This was averaged across the entire 20 year span to recieve a single value for each record.
  • Dewpoint: The dewpoint recorded on the 15th of each month for the past 20 years. This was then used with the tempurature to calculate the Relative Humidity day. This was then averaged across the entire 20 year span to recieve a single value for each record.
This was done using a python webscraper that I wrote. In total there were ~256,000 requests made to the website and then stored in a local database. Link to their page

US Census Data:

I collected data from the US Census Bureau to get the population of each county. This data was used to normalize the data for one of the visualizations. Link to their page

US Zipcode Data:

I collected data from zip-codes.com to get the county for each zipcode. This data was used to generate the county data for the visualizations. Link to their page
